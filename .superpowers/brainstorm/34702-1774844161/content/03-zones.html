What belongs on the dashboard?

The current dashboard has these sections. Which ones earn their space?

Click to select the sections that feel essential for the fixed layout. Unselected items could live on sub-pages (Alts tab, Settings tab, etc.) instead of the main overview.

1

Stats at a Glance

Win rate, rating, tournament count, champion points. Quick numbers that tell you where you stand.

2

What's Next / Action Items

Things that need attention — team submissions, check-ins, upcoming tournaments. The "to-do list" for your competitive life.

3

My Tournaments

Tournaments you're registered for — status, dates, whether you've submitted a team. Your upcoming schedule.

4

Recent Activity / Match History

Your recent match results — opponent, W/L, tournament name. A log of what you've been doing.

5

Player Identity / Trainer Card

Your username, avatar, main alt, bio. Who you are. Could be a compact card or a full section.

6

Quick Links / Shortcuts

Fast access to common actions: browse tournaments, build a team, view leaderboards, manage alts. The launchpad aspect.

7

Notification Summary

A preview of recent notifications inline on the dashboard (not just the bell icon). "3 unread: match ready, tournament starting..."

8

Achievements / Milestones

Badges, streaks, milestones earned. The fun/progression layer.
