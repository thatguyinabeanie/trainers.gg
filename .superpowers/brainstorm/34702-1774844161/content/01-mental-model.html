What's the mental model?

When a player opens their dashboard, what should it feel like?

A

Phone Home Screen

Everything is accessible from here — tournaments, teams, stats, settings. It's a launchpad with quick glances at what matters. Think iOS home screen: organized, personal, always the starting point.

B

Trainer Card

It's your identity — stats, record, achievements, alts, communities. The stuff that represents you as a player. Like a Pokémon trainer card come to life. Actions exist but they orbit around your profile.

C

Mission Control

It's action-oriented — what needs attention right now? Active matches, check-ins due, team submissions, notifications. Less about stats, more about "here's what you need to do." Think task manager meets tournament tracker.

D

Mix / Something Else

None of these capture it exactly — or you want elements from multiple. Tell me what resonates and what doesn't, and we'll refine.
