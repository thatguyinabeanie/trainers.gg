Getting back to the main site

Three approaches for the "escape hatch" from /dashboard back to the main trainers.gg site.

1

Logo → main site

Logo is a link to /
trainers.gg
🏠 Home
👤 Alts & Teams
📋 Inbox

Clicking the logo always takes you to the main site homepage. Simple, familiar pattern.

2

Quick links section

❌ Not a fan of this
Quick Links
🔍 Tournaments
🔨 Team Builder

Rejected — feels like clutter.

3

Explicit "Back to site" link

Small text link near the top
trainers.gg ← Back to site
🏠 Home
👤 Alts & Teams
📋 Inbox

Both logo AND a text link. Logo for people who intuit it, text link for people who don't.

💡 My recommendation: Option 3 (both). The logo linking to the site is intuitive for power users, but not everyone will discover it. A small "← Back to site" text link next to the logo makes it explicit without adding clutter. When in a community context, this same spot shows "← Dashboard" to go back to the player view.
