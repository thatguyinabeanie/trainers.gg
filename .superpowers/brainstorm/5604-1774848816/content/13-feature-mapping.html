✅ Every feature accounted for. All 6 tabs map cleanly to 3 tabs + 2 header actions (Settings sheet, Audit sheet). No functionality is lost. The key improvements:
  • Judge queue visible from Overview (no need to tab-switch during live tournament)
  • Top standings visible from Overview (quick glance without leaving)
  • Pairings + Standings + Judge Queue unified in "Live" tab (everything for live ops in one place)
  • Settings and Audit Log as sheets keep them accessible but out of the way
  • Registrations → "Players" with filter chips instead of sub-tabs
⚠️ One design consideration: The Settings sheet needs to be wide enough for the Tournament Structure phases editor (which has inline phase configuration with Best Of / Rounds / Timer controls). A standard Sheet width (~480px) may be tight. Options: (1) use a wider sheet (~640px), (2) use a full-screen modal instead of a sheet, or (3) keep Settings as a route-based page accessed from the header rather than a sheet. Given the form complexity, option 3 (route-based page) may be more practical — it would be at /dashboard/community/[orgSlug]/tournaments/[slug]/manage/settings and the header button navigates there directly. The key point is it's not a tab competing with Overview/Players/Live.
