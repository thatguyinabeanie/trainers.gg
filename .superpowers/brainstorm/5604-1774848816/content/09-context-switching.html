How it works: The icon rail on the left is constant — it always shows 🏠 (your dashboard) + your community icons + the ➕ button. When you click an icon, the context panel swaps to show that context's navigation. The icon rail IS the collapsed sidebar state. Clicking 🏠 while in a community context takes you back to your player dashboard.

Key details:
  • Player sidebar shows: Home, Alts & Teams, Inbox (with badge), History, your alts summary, quick links to site pages, and settings in the user footer
  • Community sidebar shows: Overview, Tournaments, Staff, Settings, plus "Live Now" with active tournaments (with judge request badge), and recent tournament history
  • Green dot on community icon = that community has a live tournament right now
  • Red badge on live tournament = pending judge requests that need attention
  • ➕ button = Request a Community (moved from /communities/create)
  • User footer = always present in both contexts, shows "Owner" or "Staff" role when in community context
