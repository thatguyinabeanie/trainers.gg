All three were things you need during a live round — they belong together. The round selector dropdown lets you review previous rounds. The real-time connection badge confirms live data. Judge queue is at the top with red styling when requests are pending. Completed matches are collapsed by default to reduce noise.

Settings & Audit Log — not tabs

These are rarely used during live tournaments and don't need tab-level prominence. They live in the header as icon buttons.

⚙ Settings → Slide-out Sheet

Clicking "⚙ Settings" opens a right-side sheet/drawer with the tournament settings form. During a live tournament, most fields are disabled anyway — it doesn't need its own full-screen tab. Same form content, just in a sheet.

📋 Audit Log → Slide-out Sheet

Clicking "📋 Audit" opens a sheet with the chronological event log. Useful for debugging but rarely needed during normal operations. Scrollable list in a sheet, not a full page.
