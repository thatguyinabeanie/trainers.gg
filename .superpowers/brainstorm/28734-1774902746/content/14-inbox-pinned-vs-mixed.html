The difference

  • A (Mixed) — everything in one timeline. Persistent items have buttons and blue dots but live alongside read info. Simpler structure, but you have to scan to find what needs attention.
  • B (Pinned + Feed) — two sections: "Needs attention" at top (persistent items on subtle backgrounds) and "Recent" below (informational feed). You always know instantly what needs action, and the feed below is just for catching up.
