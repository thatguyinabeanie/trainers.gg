The three approaches:
  • A: Summary Dashboard — two-column layout with recent results + upcoming tournaments. Full stats row. Most information-dense. Good for active players who want to see everything at a glance.
  • B: Action-First — live tournament at top, then "needs attention" items (same as bell popover but bigger), then compact stats + recent results below. Prioritizes what needs you right now. Good during tournament days.
  • C: Compact Launchpad — live tournament card, stats row, then quick-link tiles. Minimal, fast. Good if the sidebar already gets you everywhere and you don't need content duplicated on the home page.
