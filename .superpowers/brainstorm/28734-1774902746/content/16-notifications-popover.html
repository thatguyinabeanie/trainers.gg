Design details

  • 380px wide popover — wide enough for content, not overwhelming
  • Max height 520px — scrollable when many notifications
  • "Notifications" header + "Mark all read" action
  • "Needs attention" section — persistent items on subtle background cards with action buttons
  • "Recent" section — informational feed, blue dot for unread, faded for read
  • Badge count on bell — red circle with number (counts persistent + unread)
  • No tabs — just the two sections in the popover
  • No inbox page — popover is the full notification experience for now
  • Buttons are smaller — compact to fit popover size (3px 10px padding, 10px font)
