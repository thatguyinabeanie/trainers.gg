Design decisions:
  • Aggregate stats at top — record, win rate, peak rating, tournament count across all alts
  • Expandable rows — click to expand inline, does NOT auto-switch alt switcher
  • Two-column expanded — stats on left, teams preview on right
  • Teams show sprites — 6 grey squares as placeholder for Pokemon sprites
  • Archived teams — reduced opacity, labeled "(archived)"
  • "View as this alt" — explicit action to switch alt context + navigate to detail page
  • "View history" — links to /dashboard/alts/[username]/history
  • "Open in Builder" — future: opens team builder for this alt's teams
  • Badges — Main (amber), Public (green), Private (gray)
  • No page title "Alts & Teams" — just "Alts", teams live under each alt
