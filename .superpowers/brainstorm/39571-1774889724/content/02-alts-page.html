What are Alts?

Alts are player identities you use for tournament registration. Each alt can register independently, letting you compete with different strategies while keeping your identity private. Your main alt matches your account username.

Design decisions:
  • Aggregated stats at top — cross-alt performance at a glance (record, win rate, peak rating, tournament count)
  • Alt cards, not a table — each alt is a card with inline stats. More scannable than the current table layout.
  • Main alt highlighted — subtle amber border + "★ Main" badge
  • Per-alt stats inline — win rate + CP visible without expanding. Record, ELO, tournament count in meta line.
  • Public/Private toggle — small switch directly on the card, with badge showing current state
  • No teams section — teams will live in the future Team Builder sidebar item
  • Page title renamed to "Alts" — not "Alts & Teams" since teams are moving to the builder
  • Sidebar nav item should also rename from "Alts & Teams" to just "Alts"
