Two-column layout:
  • Left: Unassigned pool — scrollable list of all unassigned staff with search. Source for drag-drop.
  • Right: Role groups — Owner (non-draggable) + Admins + Head Judges + Judges. Drop targets.
  • Drag left → right to assign a role. Drag between right groups to reassign.
  • Drag right → left to unassign (move back to pool).
  • Search in unassigned — useful when you have many unassigned staff.
  • Empty role groups — thin "Drop here" zone. Not giant empty areas.
  • Remove (✕) — removes from community entirely (not just unassigns).
  • Mobile — stacks vertically (unassigned on top, groups below).
