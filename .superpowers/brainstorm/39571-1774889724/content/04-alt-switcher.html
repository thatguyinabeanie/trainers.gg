How the dashboard changes based on selection

Viewing as: All Alts (Aggregate)

  • Home — aggregated stats across all alts
  • Alts page — shows all alts with expandable teams per alt
  • History — all tournament history, all alts
  • Inbox — all notifications (always unfiltered)
  • Header subtitle — "All Alts · 48-23 · 67% WR"

Viewing as: @ash_ketchum

  • Home — stats for ash_ketchum only
  • Alts page — focused on ash_ketchum's teams + management
  • History — tournament history filtered to ash_ketchum
  • Inbox — still all notifications (account-level)
  • Header subtitle — "1,847 ELO · 67% WR"
