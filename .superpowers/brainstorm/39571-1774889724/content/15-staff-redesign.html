Key changes:
  • Drag-drop preserved — drag handle (⠿) on each staff row. Drag a person from one group to another.
  • Role groups as bordered cards — not giant colored backgrounds. Subtle color dot next to role name.
  • Empty groups — compact drop zone with dashed border (one line). Not a huge empty area.
  • Remove button (✕) — hidden by default, appears on hover. Red on hover.
  • Staff rows are compact — drag handle + avatar + name + handle + remove. One line per person.
  • Unassigned truncated — shows first N members, "+ N more" to expand.
  • Owner section — slightly larger avatar, crown badge, non-draggable (no drag handle).
  • Drop zones appear on drag — when dragging, all eligible groups highlight their drop zone with green accent.
