How notifications + inbox work together:
  • Bell icon (PageHeader) — quick glance at recent notifications. Dropdown shows last 4-5 items. Badge count.
  • Inbox page (/dashboard/inbox) — full feed with grouping, filters, inline actions. The "deep dive" view.
  • "View all in Inbox →" link at bottom of dropdown navigates to the full inbox page.
  • Bell dropdown is a quick preview — not a full notification center. Just recent items to glance at.
  • Badge count — shows unread notification count. Clears when items are read (either via dropdown or inbox page).
