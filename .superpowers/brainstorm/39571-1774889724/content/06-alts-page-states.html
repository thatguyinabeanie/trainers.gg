Summary:
  • "All Alts" view — aggregated stats + list of all alts. Click an alt row → selects it in the switcher and navigates to its detail view.
  • Specific alt view — profile card (avatar, name, badges, visibility toggle), per-alt stats grid, teams list with management actions, danger zone for deletion.
  • Teams are managed here — archive, share, open in builder. Actual team editing happens in the builder.
  • Avatar change — hover over the avatar in the profile card to edit (sprite picker popover).
  • Clicking an alt in the "All Alts" list — changes the switcher to that alt AND shows its detail view.
