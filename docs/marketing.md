# trainers.gg — Marketing Foundation

## The Three Reframes

### 1. Matchmaking, not a megaphone

Marketing isn't shouting at everyone. It's helping the specific people who'd love trainers.gg find it and trust it. If someone runs VGC tournaments and hates their current tool, they don't need convincing — they need to _find out we exist_. We're a guide, not a salesperson.

### 2. Everyone moves through the same 5 steps

Different marketing actions serve different steps. Mixing them up is the #1 beginner mistake.

| Step              | What it means        | Example action                         |
| ----------------- | -------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| 1. **Aware**      | They learn we exist  | A TO mentions us in their Discord      |
| 2. **Interested** | "Huh, that's for me" | They see the speed-tiers demo on X     |
| 3. **Try**        | They actually use it | They build a team (no account needed)  |
| 4. **Keep**       | It becomes a habit   | They register for a tournament with it |
| 5. **Refer**      | They tell others     | They share their team report article   |

Match the action to the step. Don't ask for step 5 from someone on step 1.

### 3. Trust beats reach

We don't need a million views. We need the right few hundred people, and we need them to believe it's legit. One respected TO vouching for us > 10,000 random impressions. Ads buy reach; reach isn't our bottleneck. **Trust is.**

## The Three Plays

### 🏆 TO Distribution Play

A tournament organizer is a force multiplier — recruit _one_ TO and onboard their entire player base at once. Players go where their event is hosted.

- Find 5 active TOs (in VGC Discords, on start.gg/RK9 event listings)
- The offer is concrete: _"Let me host your next event on trainers.gg — I'll set the whole thing up for you, free."_
- White-glove the experience. A TO who has a great time tells other TOs.

**Why it works:** pure trust + leverage — you're not chasing 50 players, you're winning 1 person who brings 50.

### 📣 Building in Public

Share the journey, not just the finished product. People root for a builder they can watch work.

- A few posts a week: a clip of speed tiers updating live, a slick damage calc, "added Poképaste import today"
- Tag the format/community so the right people see it (#VGC, regulation names)
- Consistency beats polish. Showing up > going viral.

**Why it works:** earns trust before anyone tries the product. By the time they click, they already feel like they know you.

### 📈 Content Flywheel

Articles + coaching create discovery that compounds for free.

- Open the article platform → players post team reports → each report is permanent search bait ("Regulation H team that went 7-2")
- Authors share their own articles → free referrals
- Coaching loop: results badge → coach profile → bookings → coaches promote their trainers.gg page → more players arrive

**Why it works:** every article is a doorway that stays open forever. Push marketing stops when you stop. This accrues.
