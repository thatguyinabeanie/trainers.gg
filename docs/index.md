Welcome to the official documentation for Battle Stadium — a **grassroots, community-driven platform** for hosting online Pokémon tournaments.

**Built by the community, for the community** — Battle Stadium empowers content creators, Discord communities, friend groups, and grassroots organizers to easily host and manage online Pokemon tournaments without corporate barriers or complex systems.

This documentation is designed for both users and developers, and is fully compatible with Obsidian (using [[WikiLinks]] for navigation).
